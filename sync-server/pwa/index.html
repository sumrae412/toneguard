Install ToneGuard on your phone

Android (Chrome) — 2 taps

1. When the "Install ToneGuard" prompt appears at the bottom, tap Install.

2. If the prompt doesn't appear, tap the ⋮ menu in Chrome → Add to Home Screen → Install.

iPhone (Safari) — 4 taps

1. Tap the Share button (square with arrow up) at the bottom of Safari.

2. Scroll down, tap Add to Home Screen.

3. Confirm the name, tap Add.

4. Open ToneGuard from your home screen.
